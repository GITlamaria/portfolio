5. The Best Way to Train for a Marathon

Tips for New Runners

  • Start slow
  • Increase mileage by no more than 10% every 2 weeks
  • Stretch

Tips for Intermediate Runners

  • Run in organized 5K's
  • Run a 10K at least once to gauge your pace
  • Stretch

Tips for Expert Runners

  • Include sprint sessions in your training
  • Add strength training
  • Make sure you rest
